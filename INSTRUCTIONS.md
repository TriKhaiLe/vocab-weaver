## ğŸ“š Features Guide

### 1. Vocabulary Weaver

Learn new words by adding them from sentences you encounter:

1. Go to the **Add** tab and select **Vocabulary Weaver**
2. Enter any English sentence containing a word you want to learn
3. Click on the word you want to memorize
4. The AI will generate a creative, rhyming sentence to help you remember the word
5. Save the entry to your vocabulary list

### 2. Sentence Shaper

Translate your Vietnamese thoughts into natural English:

1. Go to the **Add** tab and select **Sentence Shaper**
2. Enter your Vietnamese sentence
3. (Optional) Add style guidance like "formal", "casual", or specific instructions
4. The AI will provide a natural English translation
5. Save sentences you want to practice

### 3. Review Mode

Review your saved vocabulary and sentences:

- **Vocabulary List**: See all your saved words with their memorable contexts
- **Sentence List**: Review your Vietnamese-to-English translations
- Track your proficiency level for each item

### 4. Practice Mode

Test your knowledge with interactive quizzes:

- **Vocabulary Quiz**: Fill-in-the-blank exercises for your saved words
- **Sentence Quiz**: Voice-enabled translation practice
- Earn EXP and level up as you practice!

## â“ Troubleshooting

### "API Key issue" Error

- Make sure you've entered a valid API key in Settings
- Verify your API key has access to the Gemini API
- Check that billing is enabled on your Google Cloud project

### AI Features Not Working

- Check your internet connection
- Verify your API key is correctly entered
- Ensure your API key quota hasn't been exceeded

### Lost Progress

- Progress is stored in your browser's local storage
- Clearing browser data will remove your progress
- Use the **Export Data** feature regularly to back up your learning!
